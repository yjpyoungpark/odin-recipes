Spam Musubi

Ingredients

  • spam
  • seaweed
  • rice
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut spam into 1/4 inch thick rectangles.
  2. Fry spam on medium heat until brown.
  3. Splash soy sauce on spam in pan until caramelized.
  4. Form rice into a rectangle about the same shape as spam.
  5. Place spam on top of rice and wrap with 2 inch wide cut seaweed.
  6. Enjoy!
